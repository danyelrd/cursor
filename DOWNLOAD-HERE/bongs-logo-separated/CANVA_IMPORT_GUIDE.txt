Bong's Fried Chicken House - Separated Logo Layers
==================================================

FILES
-----
Bongs_Logo_Layers.pdf       One layer per page (best for Canva)
Bongs_Logo_Composite.pdf    All layers stacked on one page
Bongs_Logo_Layers.svg       Groups stay editable in Canva
elements/                   Individual transparent PNGs

HOW TO USE IN CANVA
-------------------
Option A - PDF:
  Upload Bongs_Logo_Layers.pdf. Each page is one part (circle, text,
  chicken, flames, etc.). Stack pages aligned to rebuild the logo.

Option B - SVG:
  Upload Bongs_Logo_Layers.svg and ungroup to move each part.

Option C - PNGs:
  Upload all files from elements/ and stack in order 01 to 10.

LAYER ORDER (bottom to top)
---------------------------
   1. Background (cream inner circle) (01-background-inner)
   2. Sunburst glow (02-sunburst)
   3. Large flames (behind mascot) (03-flames-large)
   4. Outer circle and maroon ring (04-circle-outer-ring)
   5. Chicken mascot (05-chicken-mascot)
   6. Fried drumstick and steam (06-drumstick)
   7. Text: BONG'S (07-text-bongs)
   8. Text: FRIED CHICKEN HOUSE (08-text-fried-chicken-house)
   9. Small flame icons (left and right) (09-flame-icons)
  10. Crown icon (bottom center) (10-crown-icon)

SOURCE
------
Procedural layers (add bongs-logo-source.png at repo root for your file)

  python3 scripts/bongs_logo_layer_export.py --input your-logo.png
